Founded in 1973, Nirmal Industrial Controls has become a leader in self-actuated pressure control valves, low-pressure nitrogen blanketing systems, gas pressure reducing stations with safety shut-off, and metering skids, earning a reputation for excellence through our commitment to quality and innovation.

Inspired by the legacy of our Late Chairman, Shri D V Desai, our success stems from hard work, dedication, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. Our talented team is focused on advancing technology and delivering outstanding products and services.
